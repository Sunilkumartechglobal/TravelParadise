Heritage & Origins

Once a wilderness inhabited by the Muthuvan tribes, Munnar was transformed into a tea hub starting in 1877. From John Daniel Munro's first lease to the establishment of the Kannan Devan Hills Produce Company, the legacy of tea is etched into every hill.
